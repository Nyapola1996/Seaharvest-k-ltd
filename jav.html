Welcome to Seaharvest (K) Ltd

Seaharvest (K) Ltd is a leading seafood processing company based in Mombasa, Kenya. We specialize in processing and exporting a wide range of seafood products including octopus, lobster, squid, and prawns. Our commitment to quality ensures that you receive the freshest and highest quality seafood.

About Us

At Seaharvest (K) Ltd, we are dedicated to providing the best seafood products. Our state-of-the-art processing facility in Mombasa adheres to the highest standards of quality and hygiene. With years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for excellence and reliability.
